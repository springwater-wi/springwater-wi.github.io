Springwater is a contemporary folk rock group based out of Madison, Wisconsin. They unify the use of classic folk instruments and harmonies with electronic instruments to create their multi-faceted sound. Springwater performs a mixture of originals and cover tunes.

Springwater is:
Ian Frei-- e. guitar, banjo, mandolin, bgv
Janie Killips-- vocals, e. bass
Allison Semrad-- vocals, guitar
Corinne Steffens-- drums, percussion

About "Winter EP":

"We've been playing music together in some way or another for a long time. Ian and Allison have been in a band together at their church since the sixth grade. Ian, Janie, and Corinne crossed paths in high school- playing many a Sufjan cover in classrooms. Allison and Janie met in Ian's basement, and at some point, Corinne and Allison were even on the same frisbee team. We are four friends with some mutual music. Eventually, we moved away from our native Madison, but we realized how much we missed the company of our shared tunes- the serendipity of music played in our parent's living rooms and the constancy of people back home.
So in December of 2013 we packed up our cars with warm clothes, borrowed gear, Christmas leftovers, and some instruments and drove two hours north to the Frei family cabin in Springwater, Wisconsin. Ian moved the foosball table out of the back room and we had ourselves a studio. This time around, we brought a few of our own songs to play- written in dorm rooms and apartments far away. We spend four lovely days chatting, reading, and playing the music that we loved so much. This EP came out of that time spent up north.".
